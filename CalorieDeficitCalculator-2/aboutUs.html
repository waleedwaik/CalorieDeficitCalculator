Aim Of This Website

Many people go into body building and dieting and waste a lot of time with minimal results because of bad dieting and misinterpreting essentials such as calorie counts. The Aim of this website is to make things easier by helping you get one small step close to your goal by helping you calculate your calorie deficit.

Precision Rate And Accuracy

We use a formula that will calculate your calorie counts with 75% accuracy on average, which is more than enough to give you a head start and a push on your progress in becoming healthier and more fit
